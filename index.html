Solentrex Email Signatures

Install in ~30 seconds (Gmail):
  1. Click Copy next to your name.
  2. In Gmail: Settings (gear) → See all settings → General → Signature.
  3. Create/select your signature, click in the box, paste (Ctrl+V).
  4. Save Changes. Gmail uploads the photo + logo for you; the logo animates on send.
Hayden: test yours first (send yourself one) to confirm photo + logo come through, then share this page with the team. If a Copy button does nothing, click in the dashed box, press Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C, and paste.
